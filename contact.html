FAQ

How quickly do you respond to messages? ▼

We try to respond to all messages within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly during business hours (9am-5pm, Monday-Friday).

Can I submit cat photos for the gallery? ▼

Yes! We'd love to feature your cat photos. Fill out the contact form and check the "photo sharing" option, or email your high-resolution photos directly to contact@catphotoapp.com. Include your cat's name and a brief description.

Do you offer professional veterinary services? ▼

We provide educational information and resources for cat owners. For professional veterinary services, health advice, or medical concerns, please consult with a licensed veterinarian in your area.

Can I use the photos on this site for my own purposes? ▼

All photos on our site are used for educational and entertainment purposes. Please contact us before using any photos for commercial purposes or outside of CatPhotoApp.

How can I report inaccurate information? ▼

We strive for accuracy in all our content. If you find any incorrect information, please let us know via the contact form or by emailing contact@catphotoapp.com with details.

Do you have a newsletter? ▼

Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter on the home page to receive weekly cat tips, adorable photos, and exclusive content directly to your inbox.

How do I adopt a cat? ▼

We're not a rescue organization, but we can provide resources and breed information to help you choose the right cat. Contact your local animal shelter or cat rescue organization to adopt!

Is there an app version of CatPhotoApp? ▼

Currently, CatPhotoApp is a web-based platform that works on all devices. Follow us on social media for updates about future app developments!
