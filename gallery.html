Photography Tips & Techniques

🌞 Lighting

Golden hour photography (early morning or late evening) provides soft, flattering light. Avoid harsh midday sun which creates shadows. Window light is perfect for indoor photos.

📐 Composition

Use the rule of thirds to create balanced, interesting photos. Get down to your cat's eye level for engaging portraits. Include environmental context to tell a story.

⚡ Action Shots

Capture your cat in motion - jumping, running, or playing. Fast shutter speed freezes action. Burst mode helps you catch the perfect moment.

🎯 Focus & Detail

Focus on the eyes for expressive portraits. Macro lenses capture cute details like paws, noses, and whiskers. Keep the background simple to make your cat stand out.
